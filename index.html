Our Mission

As your representative, I'll work tirelessly to build a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

I believe in pragmatic solutions that bring people together, not divide them. My experience in both the public and private sectors has taught me that the best ideas come from listening to diverse perspectives and finding common ground.

Our campaign is focused on creating sustainable economic growth, ensuring equal access to quality education and healthcare, protecting our environment, and making our neighborhoods safer for all residents.

About Hlomelang Lipholo

Meet Hlomelang

A lifelong resident with a passion for public service and a vision for a better tomorrow.

Born and raised in our district, Hlomelang Lipholo has dedicated his life to serving the community calls home. As the son of immigrants who worked tirelessly to provide opportunities for their children, Hlomelang learned early on the values of hard work, perseverance, and giving back.

After graduating with honors from State University with degrees in Public Policy and Economics, Hlomelang worked in both the private and public sectors, gaining valuable experience in creating effective policies and managing complex organizations.

For the past eight years, Hlomelang has served on the City Council, where has championed initiatives to improve education, expand healthcare access, protect the environment, and stimulate economic growth. pragmatic approach to governance and ability to work across party lines has earned hisrespect from colleagues and constituents alike.

In his free time, Hlomelang volunteers at the local community center, mentoring young people and helping them develop leadership skills. He lives in Riverside with his wife Michael, their two children, and their rescue dog, Max.

Key Accomplishments

Education Funding Reform

Led initiative to restructure school funding formula, resulting in a 15% increase in per-pupil spending and narrowing the resource gap between schools in different neighborhoods.

Community Health Center Expansion

Secured funding and partnerships to open three new community health centers, providing affordable healthcare access to over 10,000 previously underserved residents.

Small Business Growth Program

Created a comprehensive support program for local entrepreneurs, which has helped launch over 50 new businesses and created more than 300 jobs in our district.

Green Space Initiative

Championed the development of five new community parks and the revitalization of existing green spaces, improving quality of life and environmental health.

Budget Transparency Portal

Implemented an online platform allowing citizens to track city spending in real-time, enhancing accountability and public engagement in fiscal matters.

Affordable Housing Development

Partnered with developers and community organizations to create 500 new affordable housing units, helping families remain in the neighborhoods they love.
